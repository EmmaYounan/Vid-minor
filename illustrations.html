Child Labour in The Fashion Industry

For Design 3, I chose a sub-website from The Guardian that talks about child labor in the fashion industry. I decided to go with this website because I find this topic important, worth talking about, and I wanted to tell the story of these children in a different way than how it is told in the current website. My vision To create a design that tells a story in steps and which is supported by several illustrated animations.
