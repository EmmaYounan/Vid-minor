Illustrations

Discovering my own style

I would describe my current style, that it is mystical and somehow quiet but at the same time load. Using shades of the same color and adding to it a complementary pop of color. Using a textured brush for the shading and highlights to create a deeper look to the illustrations. Animation parts of the illustration to add some movement.

More experiments

Trying different things

Mostly I tried to experiment with different shapes, shadows, and highlights. Face shape, eyes, etc. It is still difficult for me to say that I'm totally happy with my new style but it was a nice experience to try different styles and to find out what you prefer.
